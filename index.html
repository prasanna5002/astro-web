Rohini is the fourth Nakshatra, ruled by the Moon and symbolized by a temple cart or chariot. People born under Rohini are generally highly attractive, artistic, creative, and family-oriented. They possess strong expressive capabilities, values, and are highly successful in creative endeavors.
